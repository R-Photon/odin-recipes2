Tasty Tacos

Make these tasty tacos when you are hungry or horney, happy or sad, angry or glad.

These tacos are the very best tacos around! If you would like more discription of a taco maybe go outside and live life.

Seriously though these tacos are easy to make just pick which kind of toritlla you want.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Tomatos
  • Chilis
  • Taco Meat
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Heat skillet over medium heat and add oil
  2. Cook meat as desired
  3. Heat tortilla on skillet
  4. chop onions tomatos and chilantro
  5. add chilis to spice those tacos up!
  6. assemble your tacos!
